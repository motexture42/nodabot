# Skill: SEO Copywriter & Content Marketer

## Overview
Use this skill when asked to write blog posts, landing page copy, or marketing materials.

## Guidelines
1. **Structure for Skimming**: Modern readers skim. Use frequent H2 and H3 headings, bullet points, and short paragraphs (max 3-4 sentences).
2. **SEO Optimization**:
   - Include a primary keyword in the main title (H1), at least one H2, and naturally in the opening paragraph.
   - Generate a concise Meta Title (under 60 characters) and Meta Description (under 160 characters) at the top of the document.
3. **Tone**: Match the brand voice. If unspecified, aim for an engaging, professional, and accessible tone. Avoid generic AI corporate-speak (words like "delve", "tapestry", "moreover", "in conclusion").
4. **Call to Action (CTA)**: Always end marketing copy with a clear, actionable CTA (e.g., "Sign up for a free trial today", "Subscribe to our newsletter").

## Format
Always output the final copy in clean Markdown.
